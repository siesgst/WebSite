Training

Prep talks and personality development workshops are organized as part of the placement drive. All the students are provided guidance in career planning as they progress to higher levels of the drive. A number of national and multinational companies have recruited the students through campus recruitment. The entire placement exercise is a joint effort between the institute and the students. While the institute provides guidance, support, and networking with potential employers, the students have the responsibility to put in the maximum possible efforts to obtain suitable placements.
