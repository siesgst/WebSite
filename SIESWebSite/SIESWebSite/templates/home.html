About SIES Graduate School of Technology

Instituted in the year 2002, SIES Graduate School of Technology is an integral part of SIES. It is one of the premier institutes in Navi Mumbai imparting quality technical Education. The institute is approved by the AICTE -New Delhi, DTE Mumbai and affiliated to Mumbai University.Three of its courses,namely, Electronics and Telecommunication Engineering, Computer Engineering and Information Technology have been accredited by the National Board of Accreditation (NBA). It has been certified by ISO 9001:2008 for providing quality technical education and has also been accredited by Tata Consultancy Services (TCS). With a blend of experienced and young professionals, The faculty is sincere, enthusiastic and dedicated to teaching. The excellent results and placements reflect this dynamism.
A unique course, Printing and Packaging Technology, offers students a chance to deviate from mainstream engineering and gives them an opportunity to explore a highly resourceful branch.
