WOMEN DEVELOPMENT CELL

The CWDC (College Women Development Cell) has been setup as per the guidelines laid down by the women development cell of University of Mumbai. CWDC conducts various programs and workshops to create social awareness in students about women related issues. The cell looks into the grievances of the women, both staff and students, and provides guidance and help to them.

Objectives

  1. To prevent sexual harassment at workplace and to promote general wellbeing of female students, teaching and non-teaching women staff of SIES Graduate School of Technology.
  2. To become a resource centre for women and provide a forum for exchange of ideas.
  3. To employ safety and security measures for female employees and girl students at college campus.
  4. To enhance self esteem and self confidence of women, students and staff of the institute.
