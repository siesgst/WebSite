E-YANTRA LABORATORY

E-yantra is a project supported by MHRD to spread education in robotics and embedded systems in India. Under this collaboration with IIT Bombay, the institute has established a Robotics Lab. The lab is well equipped with various Robotic kits. It aims to provide hands-on learning-infrastructure to students and teachers through application of maths, computer science, and engineering principles. The students actively participate in developing robotic applications.
