COMPUTER CENTRE

The Computer Center provides advanced computing facilities to the academic and administrative community of the college and plays a critical role in helping to keep the college at the leading edge of information technology by continuously enhancing and upgrading its facilities. The center is equipped with the latest hardware and software facilities with modern computers all well networked with 24 hour internet facility. It is not only a platform for the students to sharpen their computer skills but also provides support & guidance to the students taking up projects in the field of software.
